Time map of new cities

The timeline map that I created for the new cities in Egypt was done in 2022 using ArcGIS PRO. The map contains very important data such as the area of each city, the classification of each city, whether it is from the first generation, or from the second, third, or fourth generation, and when the construction of the city began, and the population of each city.
